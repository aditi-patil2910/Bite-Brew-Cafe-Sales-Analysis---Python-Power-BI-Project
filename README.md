# ☕ Bite-Brew-Cafe-Sales-Analysis---Python-Power-BI-Project
## 📌 Problem Statement
 
A food & beverage business wanted to understand its sales performance across items, locations, and payment methods. The dataset contains **10,000 transactions** recorded across 2023. The goal was to identify revenue trends, top-performing items, customer payment preferences, and location-based sales patterns — and present these insights in an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
 
- **Python** — Pandas for data cleaning and EDA
- **Power BI Desktop** — Dashboard and visualisations
- **Jupyter Notebook** — Development environment

### Page 3 — Time Analysis
![Time Analysis](<img width="1287" height="728" alt="Screenshot 2026-06-11 231152" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/246f7078-aa01-4914-8662-9d2122c034e6" />)
 
**Insights:**
- December peaks at $14.3K revenue and 1,255 transactions
- Revenue was flat Feb–Oct ranging $8.4K–$9.3K
- November dropped 7.51% MoM but December surged 66.62%
- Sunday is the busiest day at $20.7K — 45% above Wednesday's $14.2K
- Wednesday is the weakest day — opportunity for mid-week promotions

## 💡 Key Business Recommendations

1. Start December promotions in November
November naturally picks up — push it harder to extend the holiday spike earlier.
2. Create Wednesday special offers or combo deals on Wednesdays to lift the mid-week sales.
3. Encourage more dine-in visits by providind a dine-in discount or loyaly card to create balance.
4. Bundle Cookie with Coffee — a combo offer could boost its sales easily.
5. Reward Digital Wallet users like offering exclusive deals for Digital Wallet users could increase repeat visits.
6. Investigate the November spike — find out what caused it and replicate it in slower months.
